B1 Cells

.01 Characteristics of Living Things

Movement

Action by organism or part of organism causing a change of position or place

Respiration

The chemical reaction in cells that break down nutrient molecules and release energy for metabolism

Sensitivity

Abilitiy to detect or sense stimuliin an internal or external environment and make appropriate responses

Growth

Permanent increase in size and dry mass by increase in cell number or cell size or both

Reproduction

Process that makes more of the same kind of organism

Excretion

Scientific way of saying poopoo ; removal from organisms of waste product of metabolism, toxic materials and substances in excess of requirement

Nutrition

Taking in of materials for energy, growth and development
Plants need light, CO2, H2O, and ions
Animals need organic compounds, ions and usually water

In addition to these 7 characteristics ( MRS GREN ), living organisms are all made up of cells

.02 Cells

- All organisms are made up of cells
